The Good Husband story

About Us

Good Husband Patisserie recreates a world of celebration and indulgence — every handmade dessert made with the care of someone affectionate and dedicated.

Japanese soul · French artistry

Where two traditions meet

We blend traditional Japanese flavours with French artistry — premium ingredients shaping classics like Matcha Adzuki and Strawberry Soufflé, each with a unique twist.

Visually stunning colours and impeccable, symmetrical forms turn every creation into an edible work of art, balancing sweet and savoury into moments worth cherishing.

The founders

Foo Hou Zhang & Kaelyn Eng

Good Husband began through complementary expertise. Zhang studied traditional Japanese and French dessert techniques in Taiwan before joining Kaelyn Eng — together combining the Japanese dessert style with the pursuit of exquisite French art.

Zhang brings professional baking mastery; Kaelyn contributes an imagination for flavour and discovery. Together they craft desserts that feel as considered as they are beautiful.
